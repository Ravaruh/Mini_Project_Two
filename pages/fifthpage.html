H.E.R.

abriella Sarmiento Wilson, known professionally as H.E.R., is an American R&B singer. After initial music appearances and singles under her real name, Wilson re-emerged in 2016 as H.E.R., releasing her debut EP H.E.R. Volume 1 under RCA, followed by four subsequent EPs.
