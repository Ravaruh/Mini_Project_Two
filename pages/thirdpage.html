Steve Lacy

Steve Thomas Lacy-Moya is an American musician, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He was born in Compton, California, and gained recognition as the guitarist of the R&B group the Internet in 2015. In 2017, Lacy released his debut EP, a song-series, titled Steve Lacy's Demo.
